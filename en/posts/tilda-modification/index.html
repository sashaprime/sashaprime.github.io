Where to Find Tilda Modifications: A Curated Resource List

A hand-picked selection of tools and resources that will help turn your Tilda website into something truly unique and functional.

Jun 04, 2025
3 minute read
Русский

Tilda is great. But it can be even better! Where to find enhancements for your website?

Tilda is an incredibly user-friendly platform for quickly building websites without the hassle. You’ve probably already appreciated its capabilities — in just a few hours, you can create a site that looks great and works well. But what if you want more? Maybe you’d like a button that animates on hover, or a portfolio section that behaves differently from standard blocks?

This is where built-in Tilda features, however powerful, might start to feel a bit… predictable.

Modifications — the secret ingredients that make your site truly unique

Think of modifications like spices in cooking: they enhance your project with personality, depth, and new features. If you’ve ever wanted a special animation or unique interaction that Tilda doesn’t offer out of the box — chances are, someone has already built it. And if not, you’ll definitely be inspired to create your own after reading this article.

I’ve compiled a list of the most valuable and interesting resources to help turn your good website into something truly impressive.

Why use modifications on your Tilda site?

Imagine your site is a dish from a fine restaurant. Tilda gives you the basic ingredients and recipes, but modifications add flavor. They allow you to:

  • Stand out: Break free from template designs and make your site visually distinctive.
  • Add new features: Implement forms, animations, filters, and other functionality not available in the default builder.
  • Improve UX: Create a more interactive, memorable, and user-friendly experience.
  • Optimize your workflow: Automate routine tasks and make content management easier.

Where to find Tilda modifications?

1. Pioneers and time-tested libraries

  • Mo-Ti.ru — One of the first and most reliable resources for Tilda customizations, with solutions for nearly any task.
  • Roman-Kosov.ru — A developer’s site offering stylish and clever enhancements for Tilda projects.
  • Drik.dev — High-quality modifications including product comparison tools and advanced Zero Block effects.

2. Curated libraries and marketplaces

  • Ticode.dev — A broad collection of effects and custom scripts, from cursor animations to complex interactivity.
  • Nolim.cc — A marketplace packed with mod options, often available via subscription.

3. Sliders and galleries

  • Slider.direktoriya.site — For next-level sliders and galleries that go beyond Tilda’s built-in options.

4. Browser extensions: one-click magic

  • Annexx.cc — A browser extension that saves time and automates the way you integrate mods.
  • Voron-dev.ru — Another useful extension to streamline your daily work with Tilda.

5. Blogs, studios & creative communities

  • Mod.tistols.com — Modifications by the Tistols Studio — always stylish and functional.
  • Anku.im/blog — A compact but informative blog sharing clever Tilda tips and tricks.
  • Ilda.top — A full library of resources tailored to the Tilda ecosystem.
  • Necodim.ru — More than just mods — also a training hub focused on Tilda and other no-code tools.
  • Dsgnmax.ru/useful — A set of visually rich and polished modifications for those who want a premium look.

6. Widget libraries (for plug-and-play solutions)

  • Elfsight.com/tilda-widgets — Over 70 ready-to-use widgets: contact forms, social feeds, chat integrations, countdowns, and more. No coding required.

Tips for working with modifications

  • Master Zero Block — Most advanced enhancements are built using it. Think of it as your creative playground.
  • Test for compatibility — Always check new modifications on a test page to avoid conflicts with existing elements.
  • Back up your project — Before applying major changes or inserting custom code, save a full backup.
  • Experiment fearlessly — Try, fail, and try again. This is the fastest way to learn and discover new tricks.

Final thoughts

Modifications aren’t just technical add-ons — they’re creative tools that help your site stand out. Whether you use pre-made solutions or build your own, you’ll quickly see how flexible and powerful Tilda can be.
